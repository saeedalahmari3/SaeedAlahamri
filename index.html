Research

I am excited about using machine learning and image processing techniques to solve medical problems such as cancer diagnosis. My research focus is on topics such as medical imaging, active learning, stereology, learning with imperfect examples. Check out my CV for more info.

Biography

Saeed S. Alahmari earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science at University of South Florida (2020). Since then, he has been an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Najran University. Dr. Alahmari' research area is machine learning; his specialty is developing learning-based algorithms for labeling and learning from imperfect examples.
